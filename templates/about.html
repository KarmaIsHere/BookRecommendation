Who We Are

Bookie is an advanced book recommendation system designed to help readers find their next great read through AI-powered recommendations.

Our Mission

We aim to enhance book discovery through intelligent recommendations based on real user interactions, book content analysis, and personalization.

The Problem We Solve

Traditional book recommendation systems often rely on genre-based filtering or popularity metrics. However, these methods fail to capture the nuances of individual preferences.

How Bookie Works

  • Conversational AI: Our Dialogflow chatbot engages with users, learning their reading preferences.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): We analyze book descriptions and themes to improve suggestions.
  • Personalized Experience: Our system refines recommendations based on real-time user interactions.
